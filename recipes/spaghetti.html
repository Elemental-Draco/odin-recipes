Ingredients

  • 8 oz pasta noodles
  • 16 oz tomato sauce
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 1/2 tblsp italian seasoning
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tblsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tblsp butter

Steps

  1. First start a pot of boiling water and add salt to it. While waiting for the water to boil, finely chop the cloves of garlic.
  2. Once the water is boiling, add the pasta and cook until it is al dente.
  3. As the pasta cooks, heat a saucepan and the butter. Once melted, add garlic and saute over medium until fragrant. Add in the tomato sauce and bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to medium low.
  4. Once pasta is done, drain and add noodles to pot with tomato sauce. Add in seasonings and salt and pepper to taste.
