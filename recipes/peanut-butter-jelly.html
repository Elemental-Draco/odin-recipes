Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwhich

Description

A classic sandwhich, which as you could probably guess from the name, has peanut butter and jelly as the filling! It's great for lunch or a quick breakfast, and its so simple to make, only requiring 3 ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter
  • jam/jelly
  • 2 slices of bread

Steps

  1. First toast your 2 slices of bread in a toaster.
  2. Once toasted, spread the peanut butter on one slice.
  3. On the other slice, spread the jelly. Put the two pieces together, and boom, you have a delicious sandwhich!
