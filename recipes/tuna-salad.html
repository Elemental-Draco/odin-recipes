Tuna Salad

Description

A classic tuna salad recipe using canned tuna and some common pantry items to make a quick but delicious meal that the whole family will love! You can make this into sandwhiches as shown above, or you could also have it paired with crackers or french fries to have a great meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 12 oz can of tuna
  • 1 egg
  • 1 stalk of celery
  • 1 cup mayo
  • 3/4 cup salad cubes (or relish)
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. First you are going to boil 1 egg. While the egg is boiling, wash and finely chop the celery.
  2. While waiting for the egg to finish, drain the can of tuna well and put into a medium sized bowl with the celery.
  3. Once the egg is finished, peel and add to the bowl, along with the mayo and salad cubes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
